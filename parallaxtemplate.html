ABOUT ME

I love technology

Hello whomever may be viewing this, my name is Grace Sadahiro and I have created this personal website. I would like to thank my dear friendos Kristen, Sammy, Lauren, and Winter. Your four helped me when I was down or when you felt like I might have thrown my computer just because I didn't know I had to add a less than sign in a line to debug my code. I am so happy that I have been able to make this website (almost) the best it can be! I would most likely update this website or create more website designs in the future. Growing up, I have always had a thirst for knowledge about technology. I always wondered why my WiFi would be lost out of nowhere while I was playing Webkinz or how my Webkinz could move so fast in a game when there was some invisible connection between my computer and the sky. I'm so glad I got the opportunity to learn a language like JavaScript so that I know how to design websites before I even finish high school!

Welcome to my website. I am so happy to have been part of a program like Girls Who Code in which I could learn web development skills. I have always had a love for computer science. When I was younger, my dad bought a PC and set it up in my brother's room. My parents always typed quickly, so after school I would spend a ton of time just practicing my typing skills on typeracer. Soon enough, the iPhone came out and I was so happy to have upgraded from a Blackberry. It was lighter and there were no buttons on the screen to accidentally type "ji" instead of "hi". In my generation today, I believe technology has revolutionized for the better. There were not always programs like Girls Who Code who empower women to succeed in computer science, a male dominated field. With lots of debugging and being close to tears, I am excited to say that this website (depending on how much I've done between now and the time a Women of Warner sees this) is (probably close to) finished!
